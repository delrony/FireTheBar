This is a simple firing game, where you can fire a moving bar to reduce its size and ultimately stop it. You must fire from a rectangle firing zone. If you hit the moving bar, it gets smaller in length. If you miss the bar, it is increased. The goal of this game to stop the moving bar. The movement of the bar will be stopped when it reaches a certain length.
